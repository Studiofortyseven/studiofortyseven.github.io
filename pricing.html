Pre-Shoot Consultation

Every shoot starts with a free consultation, from which we aim to understand the audience and objectives of your project. This will set clear expectations and help us tailor the shoot to your needs in order to bring your vision to life.

The Editing Process

The images selected for editing will have: spot blending, exposure correction/lighting, colour grading applied.

Note: at this time we do not offer extensive editing.

Processing Time

Processing and editing photos genrally sees the final product delivered in about 1 week, but can take up to 2 depending on the scale of the project.

How many people can I have in a shoot?

You may have as many people as you like in the pictures. However, keep in mind more people generally increases complexity and setup time.
